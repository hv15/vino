Vino, a memoir

For many years now I have been tasting a great many wines, but until recently I had not thought to record my experiences of this. Over time one begins to develop a sense for a particular grape, region, and even vintage; but I sometimes fail to remember the name (or vintage) of a wine I tasted previously. This site exists to hold this information, including images of the label (and bottle) as well as a personal review.
